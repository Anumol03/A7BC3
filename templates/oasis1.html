About Us

Canada has always been an Immigration hotspot in the world. In the past few years, it welcomed a major fraction of immigrants to boost its market economy. The most attractive part is it granted around 4 million foreigners permanent residency in 2021 and plans more in 2022. It’s great news for us who look forward to better career growth and relocation to Canada.

Oasis is a Canada-based recruitment company providing its customers a suitable career growth and a pleasant stay in Canada. We here in oasis is adhered to giving its individuals and employers the professional, supportive and quick correspondence they desire. We accept skilled and unskilled workers in the fields of hospitality, farming, and automotive.

As a top of our service, we provide precise feedback updates to candidates and dedicated follow-ups to place them in their desired positions. Furthermore, we provide our employers with a streamlined experience in getting the right people at the right time. We prioritize trust between the candidates and employers by being a major source of expertise in the Canadian market.
